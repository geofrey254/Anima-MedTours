How we
can help you

At times you may be required to get your documents authenticated, notarized and legalised at various Government offices, embassies or consulates in Kenya before you can access medical treatment abroad. At AMT, we understand exactly what needs to be done to ensure you have all the necessary paperwork completed in a timely manner. We have streamlined our processes and established working arrangements with local partners ensuring to guarantee you a smooth process. Our trusted partners in India guide you through the processes required to access medical treatment in India including:

Before Deciding to Travel

Getting you a second opinion from a doctor/specialist in India regarding your medical condition

Getting you a quote for medical treatment services from a hospital of your choice

Advising you on any travel requirements to India

If/when you decide to travel

Obtain all the required legal documents to facilitate your travel to India including affidavits

Get your documents and records get authenticated and notarized for use abroad

Access any other legal services you may need during the process

Obtain all required approvals from relevant authorities

Get the medical VISAs for you and your companions

Book flights

Find suitable accommodation based on your preference

With pickup and drop-off to and from the airport in Delhi

Shuttle you and your companions between the hotel/apartment and the hospital

By accompanying you during your appointment with the doctors and responding to any queries you may have regarding your treatment process

Obtain quality laboratory tests/prescription medicine at affordable rates

After your recovery

Experience the best that India has to offer, helping you to plan touristic activities in India such as visits to the Taj Mahal, the Delhi National Zoo, the Mahatma Gandhi memorial and burial site and several other historical sites that you may be interested in

Arrange for your medication to be delivered to you at affordable rates depending on your post-recovery plan

Provide any other support that you may require before, during and after your stay in India
